Article on my painting on the Nepali times

Pulished on 15th Feb 2016, by Anup Rawal

This painting was motivated by the scenic veiw from my hotel room when I was on a vacation in Pokhara. This painting encompasses the wide rage of colors and mixes. This was a sunset view of the mountain around 6.PM at night. This was painted by Lhakpa Gelu Sherpa, an spiring young artist who is coming in fresh into the field of arts when every parents in N Nepal preferes their children either study engineering, doctor or else a pilot. However, this is his third art that has been selected to be auctioned and more younger nepali artists are getting involved into the field of arts. Lhakpa says "it took me almost a day to complete this painting, it encapsulates lot of my emotions that I felt while watching the mountain for hours"
